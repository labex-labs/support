We list some of the most frequently asked questions about LabEx Teams below. If you have any other questions, please [contact us](mailto:info@labex.com).

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Who can apply LabEx Teams for Educators?" icon="circle-question">

LabEx Teams for Educators is open to current teachers at accredited **colleges, universities, and high schools**. If you're actively teaching and affiliated with an institution that awards degrees or high school diplomas, you are welcome to [apply for this program](https://forms.gle/3obHecSKDScXxPhQ6).

**You must use your school email to [sign up a LabEx account](/pages/my-account/register-for-free)**, and fill out the [application form](https://forms.gle/3obHecSKDScXxPhQ6) to apply for the LabEx Teams for Educators.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What kinds of courses can LabEx support?" icon="circle-question">

LabEx Teams for Educators is ideal for courses that require an online practice environment, especially those focusing on Linux system operations and programming. With our online Linux VMs, LabEx is perfectly tailored for hands-on labs in these areas.

Whether you're teaching basic coding principles, advanced software development, or specialized topics like machine learning and DevOps, LabEx's diverse skill trees and hands-on labs can be aligned with your curriculum to enhance practical learning and skill application.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to use Alibaba Cloud Labs in LabEx Teams?" icon="graduation-cap">

You can [contact us](mailto:info@labex.com) to get the Alibaba Cloud Labs [credits](/pages/my-account/using-credits) for your students. Your students can use the teams credits to complete the Alibaba Cloud Labs in LabEx Teams.

The credits in a Teams account are shared among all members.

We will provide a special discount for the LabEx Teams for Educators. Please [contact us](mailto:info@labex.com) for more details.

</Accordion>
