## Benefits

[LabEx Pro](https://labex.io/pricing) subscribers have access to an extensive range of features.

- 6000+ hands-on labs
- Unlimited virtual machines
- 2000+ challenge solutions
- 400+ real-world projects
- Certificates of completion
- Full access to the [Labby AI](/using-labex/labby-ai) Assistant
- [Green Learn Program](/using-labex/green-learn-program)

Please note that Cloud Lab is not included in these benefits.
