## Benefits

[LabEx Pro](https://labex.io/pricing) subscribers have access to an extensive range of features.

- 6000+ Hands-On Labs
- 2000+ Challenge Solutions
- 50+ Real-World Projects
- [Certificates of Completion](/labex-pro/certificates-of-completion)
- Unlimited [VMs](/using-labex/virtual-machine)
- Unlimited-Resource Pro Cluster
- Full Access to [Labby AI](/using-labex/labby-ai) Assistant

Please note that Cloud Lab is not included in these benefits.
