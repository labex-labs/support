We list some of the most frequently asked questions about LabEx Teams below. If you have any other questions, please [contact us](mailto:info@labex.com).

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="How to use Alibaba Cloud Labs in LabEx Teams?" icon="money-bill-1">

You can [contact us](mailto:info@labex.com) to get the Alibaba Cloud Labs [credits](/my-account/using-credits). The credits in a Teams account are shared among all members.

We will provide a special discount for the LabEx Teams Users. Please [contact us](mailto:info@labex.com) for more details.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Who can apply LabEx Teams for Educators?" icon="graduation-cap">

LabEx Teams for Educators is open to current teachers at accredited **colleges, universities, and high schools**. If you're actively teaching and affiliated with an institution that awards degrees or high school diplomas, you are welcome to [apply for this program](https://forms.gle/3obHecSKDScXxPhQ6).

**You must use your school email to [sign up a LabEx account](/my-account/register-for-free)**, and fill out the [application form](https://forms.gle/3obHecSKDScXxPhQ6) to apply for the LabEx Teams for Educators.

</Accordion>
