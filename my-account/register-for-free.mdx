## Register Account

On the [registration page](https://labex.io/register), you can choose to sign up using Google or Github third-party login. Alternatively, you can register using an email and password.

## Email Verification

If you register with an email, you'll need to activate your account by clicking on the verification link sent to your email. This step is necessary to start labs on LabEx.
